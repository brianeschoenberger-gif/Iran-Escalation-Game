# Product Marketing Plan

## Product Overview
**Iran Escalation Game** is an interactive strategy simulation that lets players explore high-stakes geopolitical decision-making through scenario-based gameplay.

Players evaluate tradeoffs, respond to emerging events, and experience how tactical choices can influence regional stability, diplomacy, and conflict outcomes.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Segments
- **Strategy gamers** who enjoy political and military simulation games.
- **Students and educators** in international relations, political science, and security studies.
- **News and geopolitics followers** looking for an interactive way to understand escalation dynamics.

### Secondary Segments
- Content creators focused on strategy and current-events gaming.
- Policy hobbyists and tabletop war-game enthusiasts.

---

## Value Proposition
- **Learn by playing:** Understand escalation mechanics through direct decision-making.
- **High replayability:** Branching outcomes create different strategic paths each run.
- **Tension and consequence:** Every move has political, military, and diplomatic effects.

---

## Positioning Statement
For players who want more than arcade combat, **Iran Escalation Game** delivers a thought-provoking strategic experience where each decision can shift the balance of peace and conflict.

---

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Strategic Depth**  
   “Every decision matters.”
2. **Real-World Relevance**  
   “Play through scenarios inspired by modern geopolitical tensions.”
3. **Consequences Over Chaos**  
   “Win by balancing pressure, diplomacy, and risk.”

---

## Go-To-Market (GTM) Channels

### 1) Storefront Optimization
- Clear screenshots showing decision UI and consequences.
- Trailer emphasizing branching scenarios and high-stakes choices.
- Keywords: *strategy simulation*, *geopolitical game*, *decision-based war game*.

### 2) Social & Community
- Post short “What would you do?” scenario clips on X/Twitter, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.
- Run weekly community polls tied to in-game events.
- Share devlogs and design notes on Reddit and Discord.

### 3) Creator Outreach
- Provide early-access keys to strategy and geopolitics creators.
- Offer scenario challenge packs for streamers (e.g., “de-escalation only run”).
- Supply a press kit with gameplay factsheet and screenshots.

### 4) Academic/Educational Outreach
- Offer classroom discussion guide and scenario worksheets.
- Reach out to political science departments and student clubs.

---

## Launch Campaign

### Pre-Launch (4–6 Weeks)
- Teaser posts introducing core dilemma mechanics.
- Closed playtest clips and quote cards from testers.
- Wishlist CTA campaign across social and community channels.

### Launch Week
- Publish launch trailer + developer commentary thread.
- Coordinate creator videos/streams within a 72-hour window.
- Run “first decision challenge” social campaign with shareable outcomes.

### Post-Launch (30 Days)
- Highlight player-created scenarios and best strategic outcomes.
- Release first balance/content update with patch notes.
- Retarget interested audiences with gameplay clips and reviews.

---

## KPIs
- Wishlist-to-purchase conversion rate
- Day-1 / Day-7 retention
- Average session length
- Trailer watch-through rate
- Creator content views and engagement
- Community growth (Discord joins, newsletter signups)
